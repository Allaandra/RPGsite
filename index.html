What are Role-Playing Games?

RPGs are immersive storytelling experiences where players take on the roles of characters and shape their own adventures.

From classic tabletop games like Dungeons & Dragons to modern indie RPGs, there’s a vast world to explore!

How to Start Playing RPGs?

1. Choose a game

There are a lot of games to choose from. The most basic choice is Dungeon and Dragons, D&D for short.

2. Find a group

Tell your friends about the game. Power of imagination is very tempting, isn't it? If no one is interested, many groups exist online.

3. Learn the rules

Rules are easy to learn. You can find PDFs everywhere on the internet or watch some videos on your chosen game.

4. Start your adventure!

You can start a premade story or try a one-shot session. It's a great way to get into the game!
